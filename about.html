Who We Are

Built by Builders, for Builders

Digitroms was founded in 2022 by a group of freelance developers and digital entrepreneurs who were frustrated by the status quo. Traditional freelancing platforms were either flooded with low-quality talent, charged excessive commissions, or made it impossible for serious clients to find serious professionals.

We set out to build something different — a curated, premium marketplace where every freelancer is verified through a rigorous application process, every project is backed by secure escrow, and every interaction is designed to foster long-term professional relationships.

Today, Digitroms powers digital projects for startups, SMEs, and enterprise clients across 42 countries — from single-page landing sites to complex multi-cloud SaaS platforms.

Purpose & Direction

Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that guide every decision we make — from product features to freelancer vetting to client support.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratize access to world-class digital talent by creating a transparent, secure, and high-quality marketplace where businesses of all sizes can confidently source software and digital services — and where skilled freelancers can build sustainable, rewarding careers.

🔭

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted platform for digital services — where "hired through Digitroms" is a mark of quality that clients, investors, and industry peers recognize and respect. We envision a future where geography never limits who can participate in the digital economy.

⚖️

Integrity First

We never cut corners on vetting, never hide fees, and never prioritize platform revenue over client outcomes. Escrow-protected payments, clear milestone tracking, and dispute resolution are standard — not premium add-ons.

🤝

Community Driven

Digitroms thrives because of its community. We invest in freelancer education, client success programs, and open feedback loops. Every major product decision is informed by the people who use the platform daily.

Why Digitroms

What Sets Us Apart

We're not just another freelancing platform. Here's what makes Digitroms the smart choice for serious digital work.

✅

Rigorous Vetting Process

Every freelancer undergoes portfolio review, skills testing, background checks, and a live interview before joining. Only the top 8% of applicants are accepted.

🔒

Escrow-Protected Payments

Your payment is held in secure escrow and only released when you approve the deliverable. No milestone completed, no payment released — it's that simple.

⚡

24-Hour Matching

Post a project and receive curated proposals from matched freelancers within 24 hours. Our smart algorithm surfaces the most relevant profiles first.

🌍

Global Talent, Local Knowledge

Access talent from 60+ countries while benefiting from timezone-aware matching, multilingual support, and region-specific compliance guidance.

📊

Full Project Transparency

Real-time milestone tracking, time logs, file sharing, and communication — all inside a single dashboard. No more chasing updates over email.

🛡️

Dispute Resolution

In the rare event of a disagreement, our dedicated mediation team steps in within 48 hours with a fair, evidence-based resolution process.
